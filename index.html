Before joining Huawei, I received my Ph.D. in 2015 and became an associate professor in 2017, both at Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS). Our recent research focuses on Bayesian learning, deep generative modelling, and multiview learning, which find important applications in Web-User Interaction and Human-Computer Interaction, addressing the high-dimensional, noisy, incomplete, and multimodal data challenges. Welcome to join us!

   Research Interests Top   


  • Fields: Probabilistic machine learning, Deep adversarial learning, Multiview learning, Cross-modal/domain learning, Semi-supervised and dual learning, AutoML/meta-learning, Reinforcement learning, Multi-label learning, Multi-kernel learning, Multi-task/transfer learning, Max-margin learning, Online/incremental learning

  • Models: Latent variable models, Deep generative models, Variational autoencoders, Generative adversarial nets, Probabilistic graphical models, Dirichlet process mixtures, Hierarchical Dirichlet processes, Latent Dirichlet allocation, Gaussian processes, CNNs, Seq2Seq models, LSTM/GRU

  • Techniques: Approximate Bayesian inference, Variational methods, Expectation maximization, MCMC and Gibbs sampling, Posterior regularization, Stochastic back-propagation, Kernel methods, Matrix factorization, SGD and (conditional) batch normalization, (Bayesian) Dropout

  • Applications: Web-user interaction, Multimedia analysis, Recommender systems, Information retrieval and extraction, Human-computer interaction, Neural decoding and visual reconstruction, Distributed data mining
