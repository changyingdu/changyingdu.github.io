Meanwhile, I have broad interest in scalable probabilistic methods, e.g., deep generative models, for machine learning and data mining. Feel free to contact me for any AI research issues.

   Research Interests Top   


  • Fields: Bayesian machine learning, Deep learning, Reinforcement Learning, Multi-view/multimodal learning, Semi-supervised/dual learning, Multi-label learning, Multiple kernel learning, Multi-task/transfer learning, Large margin/support vector learning, Online/incremental learning

  • Models: Latent variable models, Deep generative models, Variational auto-encoders, Generative adversarial networks, Probabilistic graphical models, Dirichlet process mixtures, Hierarchical Dirichlet processes, Latent Dirichlet allocation, Gaussian processes, Seq2Seq models, LSTM/GRU, CNNs

  • Techniques: MCMC and Gibbs sampling, Variational Bayesian inference, Posterior regularization, Expectation maximization, Stochastic back-propagation, Kernel methods, Matrix factorization, SGD and (conditional) batch normalization, Dropout and Bayesian dropout

  • Applications: Recommender systems, Collaborative filtering, Text classification/retrieval, Image recognition/retrieval, Multi-modality data fusion, Missing value/modality imputation, Brain decoding and visual reconstruction, Image generation/editing, Text understanding/generation, Visual reasoning/question answering
