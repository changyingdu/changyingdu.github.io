I am an associate professor at the Institute of Software, CAS. I received my Ph.D. from the Institute of Computing Technology, CAS in 2015, and B.Sc. from Central South University in 2008. Currently I am working on deep generative models (e.g. VAEs and GANs) and scalable Bayesian methods for machine learning and data mining. Highly motivated students are always welcome to join our group, either as full-time graduates or as interns. If you have interest in our research, don't hesitate to drop me an email.

 Research Interests


  • Fields: Bayesian machine learning, Deep learning, Text understanding/generation, Image recognition/generation/reconstruction, Multi-view/multimodal learning, Multiple kernel learning, Multi-task/transfer learning, Large margin/support vector learning, Online/incremental learning, Semi-supervised/dual learning, Reinforcement Learning, Recommender systems and collaborative filtering, Missing value prediction

  • Models: Latent variable models, Dirichlet process mixtures, Hierarchical Dirichlet processes, Latent Dirichlet allocation, Gaussian processes, Deep generative models, Variational Auto-encoders, Generative Adversarial Networks, Seq2Seq models, LSTM/GRU, CNNs

  • Techniques: MCMC and Gibbs sampling, Variational Bayesian inference, Posterior regularization, Expectation maximization, Stochastic back-propagation, Kernel methods, Matrix factorization, SGD and batch normalization
