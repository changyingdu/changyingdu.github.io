Now I am an Associate Professor at the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences. Currently I am working on Bayesian latent variable models and deep generative models for machine learning and data mining.


 About Me


I was born in August, 1985 in Yuncheng County, Shandong Province, China and received my bachelor's degree from the Department of Mathematics at Central South University in 2008. I like reading, watching football match, listening to music and travelling at my leisure time. I am good at Chinese chess.

 Research Interests


  • Fields: Bayesian machine learning, Deep learning, Multi-view/multimodal learning, Multiple kernel learning, Multi-task learning, Large margin and support vector learning, Online/incremental learning, Transfer learning, Semi-supervised learning, Recommender systems and collaborative filtering, Missing value prediction

  • Models: Latent variable models, Dirichlet process mixtures, Hierarchical Dirichlet processes, Latent Dirichlet allocation, Gaussian processes, Deep generative models, Auto-encoders

  • Techniques: MCMC and Gibbs sampling, Variational Bayesian inference, Posterior regularization, Expectation maximization, Stochastic back-propagation, Kernel methods, Matrix factorization
